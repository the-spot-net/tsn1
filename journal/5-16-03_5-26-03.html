Friday 5.16.03
Day: 6

Well, I got up at 5:00 this morning, to get ready for my bike ride. But as it turned out, it was thundering and lightening. So I decided to call before I actually got into the shower...no one answered the phone. Lauren had the phone with her in the bathroom, but she didn't bother to answer it. Stupid girl. Stacy thought it was sweet that someone would get up at 5:00 just to talk to her.

So I went back to bed, since they didn't answer, and slept until 1:00. At that time, I decided to play some Tekken, and beat it three times on the hardest level. During that time, Amy called, while she was at the bank with her mom. She is going to come see me on the 1st of June before she goes to North Dakota for the summer. I have told Stacy about it, and I hope she is ok with it...she knows that I am not going to cheat on her, and besides, Amy is one of my best friends...oh well. I trust her, I think she trusts me. But after that, I couldn't beat the Kunimitsu that I was playing against, so I quit.

My sister came home, and had rented Catch Me If You Can, and Drumline, as well as Harry Potter (which I don't care to see) so we watched Catch Me. It wasn't as good and fast paced as the previews portrayed it to be, but it was good, nonetheless. Stacy wanted to see it too, so I am going to take it with me tomorrow and we'll watch it over there.

I called her in the middle of the movie so we could talk before we went to work today, since I didn't get to talk to her last night. And we talked, once again, until her phone went dead. She was supposed to get a new one last night, but she didn't because her and her mom were pressed for time to get home and watch Friends... . Oh well.

At work tonight, she and I worked the drive thru...it wasn't as interesting as I had hoped for. We were pretty busy, and things didn't go as I had planned them to. I did get a note from her, and she got my two letters. One of which was to explain my intentions, and the other told how I felt about her. She read them when she got home, and when I called, she was excited. We are now in agreement that we love each other. Good stuff. She had been wanting to tell it to me since we started seeing each other seriously, but didn't because of my rule. I decided I could break my rule if I had a good enough reason to, and felt that I really did. She felt the same way, that she doesn't say it unless she really means it. Wonderful.

I also found out some more good news tonight. I am going to be made into an Assistant Manager of Braum's sooner than I am supposed to. Within a few weeks from now, I will be trained and given keys and codes to become a nighttime assistant manager, plus a pay raise. Wonderful! This was the main reason that I didn't quit on the second day of the job...I knew that there was a bigger purpose that they were so interested in me working there, so I stuck it out...and look, I ended up with a good job, and an excellent girlfriend.

I am going to see her tomorrow morning, so I had best get to sleep. I will have to get up in 5 hours to get ready and such, so I'm outty. Laterz.
